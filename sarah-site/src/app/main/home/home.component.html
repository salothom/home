Established in 2017, Books & Booze is a community book club located in Austin, Texas. Founded with the idea to have great conversations about books we wouldn't nessisarily pick up by ourselves, this book club has lead its members through story lines and to many different local coffee shops, breweries, and patios. With monthly meetings, and a sprawling range of genres, there is only one rule of the club

This list comes from personal challenge to listen to at least 150 albums in 2020 with “fresh ears”, to maybe write snippets of my thoughts when listening to them, and to score them out of 7. This in part came from a desire to not only have additional excuses to reach out and talk to people whom I care about, but also to expand the genres and albums I listen to on a daily bases. This list is compiled from over 30 people. I asked them the simple question: “If you were going to make a list of albums that people should have listened to, what’s a couple albums that would be on your version?”.
